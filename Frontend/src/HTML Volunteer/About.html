Our Mission

    Our mission is to empower individuals and communities in Ethiopia by creating a centralized platform that connects volunteers with diverse agencies across the country. We strive to make volunteer opportunities accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background. Our website serves as a beacon of collaboration and social change, enabling users to discover and engage with causes they are passionate about. By fostering a spirit of community and inclusivity, we aim to amplify the impact of volunteer work, support the invaluable efforts of agencies, and cultivate a culture of altruism and service throughout Ethiopia.

Impactful Clients

    In our journey to connect volunteers with meaningful opportunities across Ethiopia, we've had the privilege of collaborating with impactful organizations such as the Red Cross Association, Care Epilepsy Ethiopia, GoEco, and African Impact. These partnerships have significantly amplified our reach and impact.

Through our collaborative efforts with the Red Cross Association, we've managed to mobilize over 2,000 volunteers in various health and emergency response initiatives, directly benefiting communities in crisis. Care Epilepsy Ethiopia, with our support, has seen a 30% increase in volunteer engagement, which has been instrumental in raising awareness and providing care for individuals living with epilepsy.

Our partnership with GoEco has ushered in an era of environmental stewardship, engaging over 1,500 volunteers in conservation projects and contributing to the preservation of Ethiopia's unique natural landscapes. Similarly, African Impact has benefited from our platform, connecting with passionate volunteers who have played a crucial role in their educational and community development programs.

In the heart of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa, our efforts have been particularly impactful. We've facilitated volunteer engagements that have directly aided over 10,000 residents, addressing a range of needs from educational support to health care, and environmental conservation. These modest yet significant statistics are a testament to the power of collaboration and the unwavering spirit of volunteerism in Ethiopia. Our platform continues to strive towards making a lasting difference in the lives of Ethiopians, one volunteer at a time.

Upcoming Projects

    Sebawi is excited to announce a range of upcoming projects that aim to enhance our platform's functionality and engagement, further empowering volunteers and agencies across Ethiopia.

1. Payment Integration: Understanding the importance of ease and security in transactions, Sebawi is set to introduce a comprehensive payment integration system. This feature will allow users to seamlessly make donations, pay for registration fees if required, and support various causes directly through our platform. This integration will not only streamline financial transactions but also open new avenues for fundraising and supporting volunteer agencies.

2. Geolocation Services: To make the volunteering experience more personalized and efficient, Sebawi will be incorporating a geolocation feature. This will enable users to find volunteering opportunities that are not only aligned with their interests but also conveniently located near them. This feature is especially geared towards increasing local community involvement and making volunteerism more accessible to everyone, regardless of their location in Ethiopia.

3. Daily Streak Feature: In an effort to encourage regular volunteer engagement, Sebawi plans to introduce a 'Daily Streak' feature. This gamification element will reward users for consistent participation in volunteer activities, enhancing motivation and engagement. Users can track their progress, earn badges, and receive recognition for their dedication to volunteerism.

4. Sebawi Stand-Alone Events in Addis Ababa: Beyond being a digital platform, Sebawi is venturing into organizing its own events. We are planning to host a series of stand-alone events, such as fundraisers and awareness campaigns, at various centers in Addis Ababa. These events will coincide with local festivals and public gatherings, providing an excellent opportunity to raise funds, increase awareness about various causes, and bring the community together. The goal is to create a real-world impact that complements our online efforts.

These upcoming projects are a part of Sebawi's ongoing commitment to innovate and enhance the volunteer experience in Ethiopia. By integrating technology and community engagement, we aim to create a more dynamic, accessible, and impactful volunteering ecosystem. Stay tuned for these exciting developments as we continue to strive towards making a positive difference in the lives of many through the power of volunteerism.
