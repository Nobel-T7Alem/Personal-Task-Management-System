About Us

Sebawi is at the forefront of connecting volunteers with opportunities in Addis Ababa. Our platform enables agencies to find passionate individuals dedicated to various humanitarian causes. From environmental conservation to education and health care, Sebawi's innovative approach ensures that every volunteer's effort makes a significant impact.

Our Impact

Since our inception, Sebawi has successfully connected thousands of volunteers with agencies, leading to transformative changes in our community. Our network comprises diverse groups, from small grassroots organizations to large governmental agencies, all working together to address crucial social, environmental, and economic issues in Addis Ababa.
